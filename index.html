Who we are

Himalayan Beans is more than just a coffee brand; it's a reflection of our deep-rooted passion for exquisite coffee and sustainable practices. Our journey began with a quest to discover the finest coffee beans nestled in the majestic Himalayan region. Committed to quality and community, we forge lasting relationships with local farmers, ensuring fair trade and ethical sourcing practices. Every batch of Himalayan Beans coffee is a testament to our dedication to freshness, flavor, and environmental stewardship. With each sip, you embark on a sensory journey through the misty peaks and vibrant landscapes of the Himalayas. Join us in savoring the essence of the Himalayas, one cup at a time, as we celebrate the art and culture of exceptional coffee.

Empowering the Nation

Himalayan Beans is dedicated to empowering Nepali youth by providing opportunities for growth and development. Through our initiatives, we aim to nurture the talents and aspirations of young individuals in Nepal, fostering a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and community engagement. Together, we cultivate a future where Nepali youth thrive, making meaningful contributions to their communities and shaping a brighter tomorrow.

Educating

At Himalayan Beans, we believe in the transformative power of education. Through our educational initiatives, we aim to ignite curiosity, inspire lifelong learning, and unlock the full potential of individuals in Nepal. We provide access to quality education, innovative learning resources, and mentorship opportunities, empowering students to pursue their dreams and build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.
